Bring serenity and beauty into your home with Florinas Lotus Seeds, perfect for growing stunning aquatic flowers in small water bowls, balconies, or backyard ponds. These high-germination, 100% natural seeds make growing lotus plants easy and rewarding. Just soak the seeds, and within weeks, you’ll witness graceful, vibrant blooms that symbolize peace, purity, and renewal.

Planting Tips:
Scarify the seed lightly, then soak in warm water until sprouting (3–7 days).
Transfer to a warm water container or pond with mud-rich soil once sprouted.
Requires 6+ hours of sunlight daily.
Bloom Time: Mid to late summer.
Ideal for: Garden ponds, water features, indoor bowls with pebbles.
